## CPS Funding Analysis FY23 to FY24
[Interactive Dashboard Link](https://ekass.shinyapps.io/CPS_Funding_Analysis/)

You can download the data here: [CPS Data](https://biportal.cps.edu/analytics/saw.dll?Dashboard)

### Background
Chicago Public Schools (CPS) publishes extensive budget information through its online dashboards. While comprehensive, the original CPS dashboard presents a large volume of data, charts, and explanatory text simultaneously, which can make it difficult for members of the general public to quickly identify meaningful funding changes across schools or fiscal years.

The purpose of this Shiny dashboard is to simplify that experience. By focusing specifically on school-level funding changes between Fiscal Year 2023 and Fiscal Year 2024, the application provides a clearer and more accessible way to understand how budgets have shifted over time.

### Why a Funding Overview (Bar Chart) tab?
The bar chart tab is designed to present funding changes in a direct and intuitive format. Users can clearly see each school’s total funding in FY23 and FY24, along with the dollar difference between the two years. This visualization removes the need for users to calculate or infer changes on their own, making the information easier to interpret and compare.

### Why a Radar Comparison Chart tab?
The radar chart tab allows users to compare the overall funding profiles of two schools at once. By displaying FY23 funding, FY24 funding, and the year-over-year change within a single visual, the radar chart highlights differences in funding scale and growth patterns. This view is particularly useful for users interested in understanding how funding trajectories vary across schools rather than focusing on a single year in isolation.
